MPTMDB

MPTMDB contains a comprehensive collection of available PTM information, which covers the target proteins, enzymes, sites, organisms, and PTM-related human diseases. MPTMDB provides such information through quickly search interface or could be downloaded from here mptmdb.zip.


Statistics

The MPTMDB contains 178,446 abstracts & 435,433 records, whose details include:

  • substrate:400,437 records
  • enzyme:71,266 records
  • site:47,596 records
  • disease:1,406
  • organisms:8

Acknowledgement

MPTMDB is available by integrating protein data from biological databases such as OMIM and published literature of worldwide researchers from PubMed. We thank all the contributors to these databases and literature.




Quick Search



User can select a field in the 'drop-down' field box, and type any keywords(eg.p53 or pka). After composing a specified conditions, you can click the "Search" button to search for relevant results. More details can be found at tutorial page.
